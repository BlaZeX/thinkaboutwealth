About

What is this?

ThinkAboutWealth.com is a minimal daily companion for those seeking financial prudence and stoic wisdom. We believe that true wealth is not just about compounding money, but compounding knowledge, patience, and character.

How it works

  • Global Reset: A new thought appears every day at exactly 00:00 UTC. Everyone in the world sees the same thought at the same time.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics. Your privacy is absolute.
  • Open Source: This project is built with static technologies (HTML, CSS, JS) and is completely transparent.
